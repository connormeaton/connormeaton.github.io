About

Hello, my name is Connor. I'm a romantically-minded luddite trying his best to work out theosis. Despite that, or because of it, I work professionally in AI/ML at Gottman Inc, mostly in affective computing and physiological sensing. I sincerely hope it helps people improve their relationships because I love humanity.

I live by the sea, but I'll soon be stewarding a homestead in the hills of Vermont with my loving wife and children. I like a lot of things, too many for my own good. Some of them are making music, reading old books, and getting lost in the wilderness. If you'd like to chat about experimental design, traditional cidermaking techniques, surfing, or what questions you’d bring to a dinner with GK Chesterton and Theodore Roosevelt, please reach out at connormeaton@gmail.com.
